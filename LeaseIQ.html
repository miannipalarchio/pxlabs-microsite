The Solution

Every obligation. Every clause.
Every lease — in days.

LeaseIQ is a Snowflake-native AI application that ingests your lease portfolio, extracts every critical clause, and makes your leases searchable, queryable, and auditable in natural language — without a single paralegal touching a document.

01 / INGESTION

Any Format, Any Source

Ingests PDF, DOCX, and scanned leases from ADLS, SharePoint, M-Files, or direct upload. OCR applied automatically to scanned documents. Your leases are indexed within hours of engagement start.

02 / EXTRACTION

AI-Powered Clause Extraction

Every critical clause extracted — rent, term, renewal options, obligations, special provisions, and risk clauses. Structured output with source citation to the exact page and paragraph for every field.

03 / INTELLIGENCE

Natural Language Q&A

Ask any question about your portfolio in plain English: "Which leases expire in Q3?", "What are our HVAC obligations?", "Do we have any co-tenancy clauses?" — answers in seconds, sources cited.

04 / TUNING

5-Question Portfolio Focus

At pilot kickoff, we confirm the five questions that matter most to your team. The system is specifically tuned to answer these reliably across every lease — before the full run begins.

05 / VALIDATION

Source Citation on Every Answer

Every output links to the exact clause, page, and lease it came from. No black box — every answer is auditable, defensible, and verifiable in the original document.

06 / PLATFORM

Snowflake-Native Architecture

Deploys entirely within your Snowflake environment. No data egress. No third-party AI processing of your lease documents. Available on the Snowflake Marketplace for instant deployment.

The 7 Questions LeaseIQ Answers

Your hardest lease questions.
Answered with receipts.

Every answer includes the exact clause and section, the document and page number, and a direct link to open that point in the original lease. Not a summary — a sourced finding.

Q1 · Termination Exposure
Which leases allow termination within 90 days — and what is the rent at risk by asset?
Q2 · Renewal Windows
Which leases have renewal options expiring in 180 days — and what are the notice requirements?
Q3 · Escalation Timing
Which leases have fixed, CPI, or step increases due in 90 days — and what is the dollar exposure?
Q4 · CAM Caps & Exclusions
Which leases have expense caps approaching the threshold — and what is excluded?
Q5 · Critical Notice Dates
What notice dates — termination, renewal, options, rent reviews — are due in 180 days?
Q6 · HVAC Responsibility
Under each lease, who is responsible for HVAC repair — landlord, tenant, or shared?
Q7 · ESG Clause Inventory
Which leases include ESG or sustainability clauses — and what are the tenant obligations?
